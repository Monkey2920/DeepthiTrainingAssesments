1.What is meant by application packaging?

 Application packaging is also known as software packaging, is the process of bundling all the necessary files, 
 configuration, and dependencies of an application into a single deployable package. This package allows for                                    easy installation, management , and distribution of the software across various environment.

2.Write down the steps involved in install MSIX?

1. Search for a Microsoft store on your computer.
2. Inside the Microsoft store search for MSIX package installer.
3. Click on the Get option.
4. After completion, click on the Yes to Allow the installation.
5. After completing the installation click on the Accept button
